The Best Movies According to Tarsit

My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers

Revolves around Earth's mightiest heroes, assembled by Nick Fury to combat increasingly dangerous threats.

Kung Fu Panda

Po the Panda, a kung fu enthusiast, gets selected as the Dragon Warrior, he decides to team up with the Furious Five and destroy the evil forces that threaten the Valley of Peace.

Shooter

Bob Lee Swagger, one of the world's greatest marksman, lives alone in the Rockies. He is recruited by a colonel to protect the President of the US. Later, he is framed for the murder of the President.
